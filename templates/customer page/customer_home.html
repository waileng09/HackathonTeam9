Leveraging Technologies To Mitigate Climate Change

Climate change resulting from poor waste disposal is a problem that needs to be addressed immediately. New and better waste disposal methods must be implemented to improve the current waste disposal methods and management. Smart disposal of waste helps prevent negative impacts on climate change and ensures sustainable living in the future. This is because it reduces the emission of high amounts of toxic gases into the atmosphere, such as carbon dioxide and methane. This will, in turn, protect and improve the environment and prevent the depletion of the ozone layer.
